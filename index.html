ACCESSIBILITY INFO

PHYSICAL ACCESSIBILITY

This event is fully wheelchair accessible, and attendees are more than welcome to work on their projects from home. There will be an accessible, gender-neutral toilet available.

SENSORY INFO

There will be slightly dimmed lights, some loud noise, and the room may be crowded and have lots of chatter. There will be stim toys and a quiet room available, however the quiet room is not physically accessible due to 3 steps leading to the room.

ACCOMODATIONS

If you have any further accessibility requests, including ISL interpretation, walkthrough videos, or further explanation, please contact us using one of the methods below, and we will try our best to accomodate you!
